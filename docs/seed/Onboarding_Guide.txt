Onboarding Guide

1. Before Your First Day
Complete all paperwork in the HR portal.
Set up your company email and Slack account using the credentials sent to your personal email.
Review the HR Handbook and Code of Conduct.

2. First Week
Day 1: Orientation session with HR (9am-12pm). Equipment setup with IT (1pm-3pm).
Day 2: Team introduction and project overview with your manager.
Day 3: Access setup for all required tools and systems.
Day 4-5: Shadow a team member and begin reviewing current project documentation.

3. First Month
Complete all required compliance training modules.
Set up 1:1 meetings with key stakeholders.
Draft your 30-60-90 day goals with your manager.
Join relevant Slack channels and recurring team meetings.

4. First 90 Days
Complete probation review with your manager at the 90-day mark.
Deliver at least one small project or contribution independently.
Provide feedback on the onboarding process to HR.

5. Tools and Access
Slack: Primary communication tool
Jira: Project management
GitHub: Source code
Confluence: Documentation
Google Workspace: Email, calendar, documents

6. Key Contacts
HR: hr@company.com
IT Support: it-support@company.com
Facilities: facilities@company.com
Your Manager: See your offer letter for contact details
