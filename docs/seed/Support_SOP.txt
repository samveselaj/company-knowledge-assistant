Support Standard Operating Procedures

1. Ticket Handling
All support tickets must be acknowledged within 2 hours during business hours.
Priority 1 (P1) tickets require immediate escalation to the on-call engineer.
Priority 2 (P2) tickets should be resolved within 8 business hours.
Priority 3 (P3) tickets should be resolved within 3 business days.

2. Escalation Path
Level 1: Support Agent - basic troubleshooting and FAQ responses
Level 2: Senior Support Agent - complex issues and account-level changes
Level 3: Engineering Support - bugs, system issues, and infrastructure problems
Level 4: Engineering Manager - critical outages and data loss incidents

3. Customer Communication
Always use the approved response templates for initial contact.
Provide ticket number in every communication.
Update the customer at least once every 24 hours on open tickets.
Never share internal system details or architecture information with customers.

4. Knowledge Base
Support agents should consult the internal knowledge base before escalating.
New solutions must be documented and added to the knowledge base within 48 hours.
Outdated articles should be flagged for review quarterly.

5. Metrics
Target first response time: under 2 hours
Target resolution time: under 24 hours for P2 tickets
Customer satisfaction target: 4.5/5.0 or above
