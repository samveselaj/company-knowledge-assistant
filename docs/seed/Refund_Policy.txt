Refund Policy

1. General
All refund requests must be submitted through the company billing portal within the applicable refund window.

2. EU Customers
EU customers may request a full refund within 14 days of purchase, in accordance with the EU Consumer Rights Directive.
Refund requests must be submitted through the billing portal.
Digital services that have been fully delivered may be exempt from refund where permitted by applicable law.
Partial refunds may be issued at the company's discretion for partially delivered services.

3. US Customers
US customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase for unused services.
Once a service has been activated and used, refunds are available only at the discretion of customer support.

4. Enterprise Customers
Enterprise customers on annual contracts should refer to Section 12 of their Master Service Agreement for refund terms.
Early termination fees may apply.

5. Processing
Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days.
Refunds are issued to the original payment method unless otherwise arranged.
