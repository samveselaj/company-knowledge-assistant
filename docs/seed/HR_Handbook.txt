HR Handbook

1. Employment
All new employees undergo a probation period of 90 days.
During probation, either party may terminate employment with 2 weeks notice.

2. Leave Policy
Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Part-time employees receive leave on a pro-rata basis.
Contractors receive 10 unpaid leave days per year.
Unused leave may be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days, subject to manager approval.

3. Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements require VP-level approval.
Remote employees must maintain core hours of 10am-3pm in their local timezone.

4. Benefits
Full-time employees are eligible for health insurance after 30 days of employment.
The company provides a 401(k) match of up to 4% of base salary.
Professional development budget of $2,000 per year is available after 6 months.

5. Code of Conduct
Employees are expected to maintain professional behavior at all times.
Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation are strictly prohibited.
Violations should be reported to HR or through the anonymous reporting system.
